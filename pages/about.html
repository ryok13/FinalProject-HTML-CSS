Who we are

At Georgian Retro Games, we’re more than just a store—we’re a celebration of gaming history. The Nintendo Game Boy is more than a console; it’s a symbol of creativity, fun, and memories for millions of players. Our mission is to ensure that the legacy of this legendary system continues to thrive.

We’re passionate about retro gaming and dedicated to offering:

  • A Commitment to Quality: Every console, game, and accessory is thoroughly inspected and tested so you can enjoy worry-free gaming.
  • A Community for Gamers: Georgian Retro Games isn’t just about products; it’s about connecting with others who share a love for the classics.
  • Expertise You Can Trust: With years of experience in retro gaming, we know the Game Boy inside and out. We only sell products that meet our high standards for quality and authenticity.

We understand the magic of the Game Boy—it’s the thrill of discovering a new game, the challenge of a tricky level, and the nostalgia of sharing these moments with friends. That’s why we strive to create a store that honors this legacy while providing gamers with everything they need to bring those memories back to life.

Thank you for choosing Georgian Retro Games as your retro gaming destination. Let’s keep the 8-bit adventures alive!

Our story

Georgian Retro Games began as a passion project. Our founder, a lifelong gamer, discovered their childhood Game Boy tucked away in storage one winter. Turning it on after years, they were transported back to the simpler times of 8-bit adventures and endless hours spent with classics like Super Mario Land and Pokémon Red.

The joy of reliving those memories sparked an idea: what if this nostalgia could be shared with others? This passion grew into a mission to make the Nintendo Game Boy accessible to both seasoned retro gamers and those discovering it for the first time.

Our promise for the future

Georgian Retro Games wishes to become place made to further connect people with the industry of gaming, as well as connect them with the past this industry has, and the development it's had progressively along the years. Our promise is to further improve the accessibility of the people when it comes to obtaining these kinds of consoles, which have become so rare as time went on.

We also promise to help people connect with the history of gaming from it's very core, it's ups and downs, and it's evolution throughout the years. We also wish to bring back memories to those who have experienced these consoles before, and wish to own one again, making it simpler than ever, and much cheaper as well, since we are well aware of how expensive obtaining retro games and consoles can get due to their rarity.
